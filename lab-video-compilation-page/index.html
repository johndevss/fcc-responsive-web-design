Compilation of Popular OPM Songs

Here is a collection of some of the most popular Original Pilipino Music (OPM) songs.

Tibok - Earl Agustin

One of my favorite OPM songs! It shows the feeling of falling in love.

Multo - Cup of Joe

A hauntingly beautiful song that captures the essence of longing and memories.

Migraine - Moonstar88

A song that expresses the pain and confusion of a troubled mind when falling in love.
