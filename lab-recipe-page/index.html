Adobong Sitaw Recipe

A Filipino dish made with string beans are cooked using the inadobo method. It is savory, slightly tangy, and packed with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© the garlic and onion until fragrant and translucent.
  2. Add the string beans and cook for about 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Pour in the soy sauce and vinegar. Do not stir immediately; let it simmer for a minute to cook off the vinegar's strong taste.
  4. Add water, cover the pan, and let it simmer for about 10-15 minutes or until the string beans are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Adjust the seasoning as needed.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice.
